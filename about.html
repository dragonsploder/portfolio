About Me

Hi! I am Joshua Bourgeot, a Computer Science and Computational Mathematics major at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) with a deep passion for blending technical expertise and creative problem-solving. My academic interests range from low-level programming and assembly to artificial intelligence and mathematical modeling, complemented by hands-on experience through professional projects and internships.

At RIT, I actively contribute to the university's vibrant community. As a member of RIT Players, I bring both live stage acting experience and organizational skills as a Production Manager, where I oversee the behind-the-scenes coordination of student-led theater productions. I am President and Co-Founder of the Retro Games Club, where we celebrate the ingenuity of classic gaming through community and technical exploration.

From developing a Nintendo emulator in Rust to upgrading enterprise IT systems, I aim to bridge my technical expertise with a creative mindset to tackle complex problems. Looking ahead, I am eager to contribute to projects that integrate my interests in computing, mathematics, and creative expression.

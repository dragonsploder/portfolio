Well

Overview

Well is a lightweight game engine written in C with OpenGL. It includes a custom rendering pipeline, with lighting and shadow support. I designed it to render 3D objects into a pixart-esq style. UI and physics support have been integrated with the Nuklear and ODE libraries respectively. This was a complex project, but it taught me a lot about rendering pipelines, and how things actually appear on screen at a very low level.

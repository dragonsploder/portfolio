Dungeon Gen

Overview

This is an AI ASCII Dungeon Generator. It generates dungeons similar to those found in Rogue, the original roguelike. The model is pretty straightforward, just a single MLP (MultiLayer Perceptron). However it works pretty well. Most of the dungeons have a few mistakes, but for the size of the model they are still impressive.

This is one of my first projects that has anything to do with deep learning and AI. While it is pretty simple, I learned a lot about the fundamentals and hope this can be a jump off point for future projects.

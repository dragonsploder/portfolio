Die of the Dungeon

Overview

My second game jam game, this time for the GMTK Game Jam 2022, was certainly more ambitious than the first. Not only did I do all the design, programming, and art, but I did the music for this one as well. The theme of the jam was "Roll of the Dice" and in the 48 hours available to me I made a fun little tile based pixel art puzzle game. The player must choose between different ways to influence dice rolls to move Gill the Generic Adventurer to the treasure.

Like the first jam, I was very happy with how this game turned out. Having a bit more experience with Godot at this point, I was able to make a more complex and unique game which had a pretty clever take on the theme.

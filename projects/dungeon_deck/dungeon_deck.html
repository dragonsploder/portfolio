Dungeon Deck

Overview

In the fall semester of my junior year at RIT, I took an ATARI 2600 programming class. It quickly become one of my favorite classes. The ATARI 2600 came out in 1977 and was the first popular home console. Home to classics from Combat to Pitfall, it was revolutionary to the gaming industry. It was also encredibly hard to program for due to it's limited hardware. Throughout the class and creation of this game, I not only learned how to work with a complex, technical system, but also how to boil game design down to its fundamental and most important concepts. Dungeon Deck is a bit of an outlier in the ATARI 2600 family, but I hope you can enjoy it nonetheless.

Skills

  • Game Design
  • Iterating on feedback to long projects

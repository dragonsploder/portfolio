Self Control

Overview

Self Control was my submission to the first game jam I ever participated in: The GMTK Game Jam 2020. The theme of the jam was "Out Of Control" and each team had 48 hours to make the completed product. I ended up creating a simple platformer where the jump was out of your control and instead would trigger at random times, designated by a bar at the top of the screen. I was a solo team for this jam, which means I did the design, programming, and art all by myself (although my dad helped with the music). This was also my first full project using Godot, so a good chunk of the time was spent learning how to use the amazing game engine.

This project was great fun to work on, and it was amazing to actually release something. In fact the best part happened after the jam, where I got to play other games submitted, give, and receive feedback.

Dynamo

Overview

Skills

Dynamo is an audio effects plugin, specifically a flanger. I have always enjoyed dabbling in music production, and this project was a fun intersection of programming and music for me. The VST plugin can be used in most DAWs (digital audio workstations) and will produce a "sweepy, whooshing" effect on any audio that is put through it.

A fun little extra part of this project: I custom modeled and rendered the knobs in Blender for a fun artistic touch.

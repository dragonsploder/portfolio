Markov Chain Text Generator

Overview

Markov chains are a process for describing sequences of events based on the probability of previous events. They can be used for rudimentary text generation based on data from a large corpus. This is a simple python implementation of such an idea, based on a Caves of Qud talk of the same concept. While the generated text is usually nonsensical, occasional a fun and interesting sentence is generated.
